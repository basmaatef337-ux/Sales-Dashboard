# Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard developed using Microsoft Excel.  

The dashboard provides a detailed analysis of sales performance across regions, channels, categories, and sales representatives. It enables business stakeholders to monitor revenue, profit, and sales trends effectively.

---

## 🖼 [Dashboard Preview](Project1Sales Dashboard.JPG)

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze total Net Sales and Profit
- Evaluate sales performance by region and channel
- Monitor monthly sales trends
- Identify top-performing sales representatives
- Understand product category contribution
- Analyze sales distribution across performance bands

- Total Net Sales: $188,491.80  
- Total Profit: $40,146.63

## 📈 Dashboard Components

### 1️⃣ Net Sales by Region & Channel
- East region shows strong performance.
- Distributor and Online channels contribute significantly.

### 2️⃣ Monthly Net Sales Trend
- Sales peak observed in June.
- Seasonal fluctuation visible across quarters.

### 3️⃣ Sales Band Distribution
- Majority revenue generated from High sales band.
- Medium segment has growth potential.

### 4️⃣ Profit by Category
- Electronics category dominates profit share.
- Accessories contribute smaller but stable margin.

### 5️⃣ Salesperson Net Sales
- Sara and Lina are top contributors.
- Opportunity to improve performance of lower-tier sales reps.

## 💡 Business Insights

- Revenue concentration in specific regions suggests geographic dependency.
- Electronics category drives majority profit contribution.
- Performance variance across salespersons indicates need for targeted incentive plans.
- Sales seasonality suggests marketing alignment opportunities.

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

- Expand marketing in high-performing regions.
- Implement incentive program for mid-performing sales reps.
- Optimize low-performing sales channels.
- Increase focus on high-margin product categories.
